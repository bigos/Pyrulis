** Brackets
http://softwareengineering.stackexchange.com/questions/195090/are-square-brackets-and-curly-braces-in-clojure-still-s-expressions
Different bracket shapes are a syntax sugar. But the convention is to use:

#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
[1 2 3] for vectors
(1 2 3) for lists
{:a 1 :b 2} for hashes
#{1 2 3} for sets
#+END_EXAMPLE
